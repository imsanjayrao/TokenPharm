TokenPharm




In today's modern world our community has to deal with people who take drugs without prescription for personal use and not issued by a doctor and this is a big problem which could result in unthinkable situation by being under the influence. Many solutions have come into light reagarding the same matter where professionals and people suggest that effective coordination among enforcement agencies: Coordination in illicit drug control is the function of the Narcotics Control Bureau, Department of Revenue. ...
Sharing of intelligence: Many state and central agencies work in isolation and many more solutions but we aim to go for the head and deal with the problem right from the start when the raw materials get produced and for that we have created this blockchain based token management drug supply chain system which starts from the people who give raw material and ends with the patient and includes everyone in between including the transporters, infirmaries, doctors etc.
Our project aims to utilize immutability and the ability to mint tokens offered by a blockchain to enforce accountability and mitigate drug flow into illicit channels. This project is the broad-scope solution to the leak of drugs into illegal media due to flaws and under-the-table deals between players.
